Haruki Murakami

This is my favorite author, Haruki Murakami. Why do I like him so much?

Reasons I like Murakami:

  • He writes about the everyday and the fantastic.
  • His obsessions are always present (cats, food, wells, etc.).
  • He likes to run and sees it as meditative, just like me!
